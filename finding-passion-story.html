Finding Your Passion in High School

- figuring out what your main activity is through High School -

During high school, many students join clubs that they are interested in. Some clubs are fun and small and other clubs, you invest most of your time in. Throughout High School you should find 1 or 2 clubs that you want to do and pursue it. By the end of your high school career you should hopefully have a leadership role in that club. This will show your commitment and help make you more responsible.
