How to still have fun through High School

Everybody is different. Having fun could mean going to parties, hanging out with your friends, or just staying home watching your favorite shows. Even though having fun is a good break from all the stress of school, you have to make sure that it wont afffect your school performance. To clarify, you should'nt be hanging out with your friends every weekend if you need to study for the test or finish a project. But on the other hand, you shouldn't be doing school work all weekend and also need to take a break so you won't wear yourself out. Even if that means taking an hour break to read or watch Netflix.
