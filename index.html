About Me

My name is Anagha and I'm a sophomore in high school. I know high school is hard, and I created this website to help students ease off some of their stress and know that other people are going through the same things.

What to do

In this website you can read stories written by me and relate to them. You can post/send questions and get responses from me or other followers, and you can answer other's questions. At High School Help you can get and give advice to make your life a little bit easier.

What this is about

At High School Help, hopefully these stories will help you through your struggles and help solve your problems. Come here to take a break from school and read students stories and experiences of their time during high school.
